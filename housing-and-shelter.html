Housing and Shelter

Housing and shelter are very important for protecting us from weather like rain, heat, and cold. It also gives us a safe place to live and rest.

People build different types of houses depending on the climate, materials available, and culture.

Example: In cold places, houses are made with thick walls to keep the warmth inside, while in hot places, houses often have large windows to let in air and keep cool.
