Spatial Relationships

Spatial relationships describe how objects are positioned in space in relation to each other. It helps us understand where things are — like above, below, next to, or inside.

Why are Spatial Relationships Important?

Knowing spatial relationships helps us describe locations, understand directions, and organize objects correctly.
