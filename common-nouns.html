Common Nouns

A common noun is the name of a person, place, thing, or animal. We use common nouns to talk about everyday things.

Examples of common nouns are: boy, girl, city, dog, apple, and school.

Example:
"The boy is playing in the park. The dog is running fast."

Diagram: Types of Common Nouns

  • Person: boy, teacher, doctor
  • Place: school, park, city
  • Thing: book, apple, pen
  • Animal: dog, cat, bird

Flowchart: How to Identify a Common Noun

Word → Is it a person, place, thing, or animal? → Yes → Common Noun
No → Not a Common Noun
