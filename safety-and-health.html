Safety and Health

Safety means protecting ourselves from harm or injury, and health means being free from sickness. Both are important to live a happy and active life.

We can stay safe and healthy by following simple rules and habits every day.

Example: Wearing a helmet while riding a bicycle helps protect your head from injuries.
