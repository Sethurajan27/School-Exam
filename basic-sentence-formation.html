Basic Sentence Formation

A sentence is a group of words that expresses a complete thought. It usually has a subject and a predicate.

Subject: Who or what the sentence is about.
Predicate: What the subject does or is.

Examples:
- The cat (subject) sleeps (predicate).
- She (subject) runs fast (predicate).
- Birds (subject) sing songs (predicate).
