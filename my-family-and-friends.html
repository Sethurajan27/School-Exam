My Family and Friends

Family and friends are people who care about us and share happy moments. Family is those we live with and love, while friends are people we enjoy spending time with.

Both family and friends help us feel safe, loved, and supported.

Example:
I have a loving family of four, and I have many friends at school. We play games, share stories, and help each other.
