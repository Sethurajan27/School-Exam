Introducing Oneself

Introducing oneself means telling others who you are in a polite and friendly way.

Example of Introducing Yourself:
"Hello! My name is Sara. I am 6 years old. I study in Grade 1. Nice to meet you!"

Flowchart: Steps to Introduce Oneself

Greet → Say Your Name → Share Age or Grade → Say Something Nice → Smile

Parts of an Introduction

  • Greeting: Say hello or hi
  • Name: Tell your name
  • About You: Share a little information
  • Closing: Say something nice
