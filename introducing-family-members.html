Introducing Family Members

Family is a group of people who love and care for each other. We have many family members like parents, siblings, grandparents, uncles, and aunts.

When we introduce our family, we tell who they are and how they are related to us.

Example:

"This is my father. His name is Raj. This is my mother, Meena. I have a younger sister, Riya."
