Food and Nutrition

Food is what we eat to stay alive, grow, and stay strong. It gives our body energy and helps it to work properly. Nutrition is the goodness in food that helps our body stay healthy and fight off sickness.

Example: Fruits and vegetables are rich in vitamins. Milk gives us calcium to build strong bones and teeth.

Types of Food

  • Energy-giving foods (e.g., rice, bread)
  • Body-building foods (e.g., milk, eggs)
  • Protective foods (e.g., fruits, vegetables)

Good Food Habits

  • Eat fresh and clean food
  • Wash hands before eating
  • Chew food properly
  • Drink plenty of water
