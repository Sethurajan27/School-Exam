About Myself

"About Myself" means telling others who I am, what I like, and what makes me special. It helps me share my thoughts, feelings, and experiences with others.

It is important to understand ourselves so we can grow, learn new things, and be happy.

Example:
My name is Alex. I am 6 years old. I love painting and playing with my friends. I have a pet dog named Max.
