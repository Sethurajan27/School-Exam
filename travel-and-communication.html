Travel and Communication

Travel means moving from one place to another. Communication means sharing information with others. Both help us stay connected with family, friends, and the world.

People travel by walking, bicycle, car, bus, train, airplane, and ship. Communication can be done by talking, writing letters, using phones, or the internet.

Example: When you visit your grandparents in another city, you travel by train. You can also call them on the phone to talk before you go.
