Rain Rain Go Away

"Rain Rain Go Away" is a popular nursery rhyme that children sing when it rains. It is often sung when children want to go outside and play, but the rain is stopping them.

The rhyme shows how children express their feelings in a fun and musical way. It teaches them about weather and emotions. Even though rain is useful for plants and animals, children sometimes wish it would stop so they can go outside.

Example:
Rain, rain, go away,
Come again another day,
Little Johnny wants to play!
