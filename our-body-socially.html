Our Body Socially

Our body helps us connect and communicate with others. We use our eyes to see, ears to listen, mouth to speak, and hands to greet.

Being social means sharing, caring, and respecting others. This helps build good friendships and makes us feel happy.

Example:
When I greet my friends with a smile and say “hello,” I am using my body to be social.

Flowchart: How We Use Our Body Socially

See → Listen → Speak → Smile and Greet → Make Friends

Diagram: Body Parts Used for Social Interaction

  • Eyes - to see expressions
  • Ears - to listen carefully
  • Mouth - to talk kindly
  • Hands - to wave or shake hands
  • Face - to smile and show emotions

Social Well-being Formula

Social Happiness = Kind Words + Friendly Actions + Smiles
