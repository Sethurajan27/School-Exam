Food and Clothes

Food and clothes are two important things we need every day. Food gives us energy to work and play, while clothes protect us and keep us comfortable.

Different kinds of food provide different nutrients. Similarly, clothes are made from different materials to suit different weather and occasions.

Example:
We eat fruits and vegetables to stay healthy, and we wear cotton clothes in summer to stay cool.

Flowchart: Choosing Food and Clothes

Need → Weather/Season → Type of Food & Clothes → Healthy Body + Comfort

Diagram: Types of Food and Clothes

  • Food: Fruits, Vegetables, Grains, Dairy, Meat
  • Clothes: Cotton, Wool, Silk, Synthetic

Health and Comfort Formula

Healthy Body = Balanced Food + Suitable Clothes + Clean Environment
