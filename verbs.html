Verbs

A verb is an action word. It tells what someone or something does.

Examples of verbs are: run, jump, eat, sleep, and play.

Example:
"The cat runs fast. The children play in the park."

Diagram: Types of Verbs

  • Action Verbs: run, jump, eat, write
  • Helping Verbs: is, are, was, were
  • Linking Verbs: am, is, are, become
