Weather and Seasons

Weather means the condition of the air outside at a certain time and place. It can be sunny, rainy, cloudy, windy, or snowy.

Seasons are different times of the year that have typical weather patterns. There are four seasons:

  • Spring: Flowers bloom and the weather becomes warmer.
  • Summer: It is usually hot and sunny.
  • Autumn (Fall): Leaves change color and fall from trees.
  • Winter: It is cold and may snow in some places.
Example: In summer, we wear light clothes and enjoy sunny days, while in winter, we wear warm clothes and sometimes play in the snow.
