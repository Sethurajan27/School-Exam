Air and Water

Air and water are two of the most important natural resources for all living things. Air is the invisible gas around us that we breathe. Water is a liquid found in rivers, lakes, and oceans that all living beings need to survive.

Both air and water are essential for plants, animals, and humans to live healthy lives.

Example: We breathe air to live, and we drink water to stay healthy and clean.
