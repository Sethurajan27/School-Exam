My Family

Family is a group of people who love and care for each other. They live together and support one another. Families can be big or small.

Our family members include parents, brothers, sisters, grandparents, uncles, aunts, and cousins.

Example:
My family has five members: my father, mother, two brothers, and me. We enjoy spending time together and helping each other.
