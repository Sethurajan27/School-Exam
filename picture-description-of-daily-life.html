Picture Description of Daily Life

Describing a picture of daily life helps us tell others what is happening in the scene. We look carefully and talk about the people, actions, places, and feelings shown.

When we describe daily life pictures, we use simple sentences to explain what we see.

Example:
“In the picture, a boy is brushing his teeth in the morning. His mother is preparing breakfast in the kitchen. Outside, the sun is shining, and birds are flying.”

Flowchart: Steps to Describe a Picture

Look Carefully → Notice People and Actions → Describe Place → Use Simple Sentences

Diagram: What to Include in Picture Description

  • People and What They Are Doing
  • Objects and Places
