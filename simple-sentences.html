Simple Sentences

A simple sentence is a group of words that expresses a complete thought. It has a subject (who or what the sentence is about) and a predicate (what the subject does or is).

Parts of a Simple Sentence

  • Subject: The person, animal, or thing the sentence is about.
  • Predicate: The action or state of the subject.
Example:
The cat (subject) sleeps (predicate).
