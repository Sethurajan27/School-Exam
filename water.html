Water

Water is a very important natural resource essential for all living beings. It is used for drinking, cooking, cleaning, farming, and many other activities. Without water, life cannot exist.

Water can be found in rivers, lakes, oceans, and even underground. It changes its form through evaporation, condensation, and precipitation.

Example: When the sun heats up water in rivers and lakes, it evaporates and forms clouds. Later, this water comes down as rain.

Water Cycle Flowchart

Evaporation → Condensation → Precipitation → Collection → Evaporation
