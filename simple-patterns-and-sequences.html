Simple Patterns and Sequences

Patterns and sequences are fun and easy to understand!

When something repeats in a special order, that is called a pattern. Sequences are numbers or objects that follow a certain rule.

🔴 🔵 🔴 🔵 🔴 🔵 → What comes next? 🔴!

🍎 🍌 🍎 🍌 🍎 🍌 → Yes! It’s a banana (🍌) next.

1, 2, 3, 4, 5, __? → The next number is 6.

10, 20, 30, __? → The pattern is +10, so next is 40.

💡 Tip: Look closely at what is repeating. Use colors, shapes, or numbers to make your own patterns!

You can make patterns using your toys, colors, or blocks. Try to continue the pattern or even make your own sequence!

Learning patterns helps us with thinking, prediction, and problem-solving!
