Everyday Objects

Everyday objects are things we use daily in our homes and schools. These objects help us in many ways and make our life easier.

Some common everyday objects are: a chair, a table, a pen, a book, a cup, and a clock.

Example:
"I write with a pen. I sit on a chair. The clock shows the time."

Diagram: Categories of Everyday Objects

  • Furniture: chair, table, bed
  • Stationery: pen, pencil, eraser
  • Kitchen Items: cup, plate, spoon
  • Clocks & Watches: clock, watch
