Word Formation

Word formation is the way new words are made from other words. It helps us to make more words to express ideas better.

There are many ways to form new words, such as:

  • Adding a prefix (a small part added to the beginning of a word)
  • Adding a suffix (a small part added to the end of a word)
  • Joining two words to make one new word
Examples:
  • Happy → Unhappy (prefix: un-)
  • Care → Careful (suffix: -ful)
  • Sun + Flower = Sunflower (joining two words)
