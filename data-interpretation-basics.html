Data Interpretation Basics

Data interpretation means looking at information and understanding what it tells us. It helps us learn things by reading tables, charts, or graphs.

Why is Data Interpretation Important?

It helps us answer questions like “Which is the most popular fruit?” or “How many children like apples?” by reading data carefully.

Example:

Look at the table below showing how many fruits different children ate in a week.

Fruit Number of Fruits Eaten
Apples 5
Bananas 3
Oranges 4

From this data, we can see that apples were eaten the most (5 fruits), bananas the least (3 fruits), and oranges are in between (4 fruits).

By looking at tables like this, we can understand and compare information easily.
