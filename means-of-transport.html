Means of Transport

Means of transport are the ways we use to move people and goods from one place to another. They help us travel faster and carry heavy things easily.

There are different means of transport: by land, water, and air.

  • Land Transport: Bicycle, Car, Bus, Train, Motorcycle
  • Water Transport: Boat, Ship, Ferry
  • Air Transport: Airplane, Helicopter
Example: When you go to school by bus, you are using land transport. When people travel to faraway countries, they usually fly by airplane.
