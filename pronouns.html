Pronouns

Pronouns are words used in place of nouns to avoid repeating the same words again and again.

They help us talk about people, animals, or things without naming them every time.

Types of Pronouns

  • Personal Pronouns: I, You, He, She, It, We, They
  • Possessive Pronouns: Mine, Yours, His, Hers, Ours, Theirs
  • Demonstrative Pronouns: This, That, These, Those
  • Interrogative Pronouns: Who, What, Which
Example:
Instead of saying "Sarah is happy. Sarah likes to play," we say "She is happy. She likes to play."
