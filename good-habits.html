Good Habits

Good habits help us stay healthy, happy, and successful. They make our life better and help us become good people.

Some good habits include brushing teeth twice a day, washing hands before eating, studying regularly, and being kind to others.

Example:
Brushing your teeth every morning and night keeps your teeth clean and prevents cavities.
