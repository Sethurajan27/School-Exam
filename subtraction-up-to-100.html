Subtraction up to 100

Now let’s learn how to subtract bigger numbers — up to 100!

We use the minus sign (−) and subtract tens or ones to find the answer.

🍬 80 − 25 = 55

🧸 63 − 12 = 51

🚗 95 − 40 = 55

🎲 72 − 36 = 36

📚 100 − 45 = 55

💡 Tip: Break down numbers into tens and ones to subtract easily.
Example: 64 − 32 → 60 − 30 = 30 and 4 − 2 = 2 → So, 64 − 32 = 32

Keep practicing with your notebook or toys. You will become a subtraction star! 🌟
