Singular and Plural

Singular means one.
Plural means more than one.

Singular to Plural

  • Singular: One apple
  • Plural: Two or more apples (add "s")

To make a noun plural, usually add "s" or "es" at the end of the word.

Flowchart: Making Plural Nouns

Start with Singular → Check Ending → Add "s" or "es" → Plural Form

Examples:
1. Book → Books
2. Box → Boxes
3. Baby → Babies
