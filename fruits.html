Fruits

Fruits are the sweet and juicy parts of plants that we eat. They are full of vitamins and help keep us healthy. Different fruits grow in different seasons and places.

Some common fruits are apples, bananas, mangoes, oranges, and grapes.

Example:
"An apple is round and red or green. It is sweet and crunchy. Bananas are long and yellow, soft and sweet inside."
