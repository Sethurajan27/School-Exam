Our Helpers

Helpers are special people who work to make our lives better and safer. They help us in many ways at home, school, and in the community.

Some helpers are doctors who keep us healthy, teachers who help us learn, police officers who keep us safe, and firefighters who put out fires.

Example:
When someone is sick, a doctor helps them feel better by giving medicine and advice.
