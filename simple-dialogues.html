Simple Dialogues

Dialogues are short conversations between two or more people. They help us practice speaking and listening.

Example Dialogue:

Ravi: Hello, Sita! How are you?
Sita: I am fine, thank you. And you?
Ravi: I am doing well. What did you do today?
Sita: I went to the park with my family.

Flowchart: How a Dialogue Happens

Start Conversation → Person 1 Speaks → Person 2 Responds → Continue Talking → End Conversation

Parts of a Dialogue

  • Speaker: The person who is talking
  • Speech: The words spoken
  • Response: Reply to what was said
  • Turn-taking: People take turns to talk
