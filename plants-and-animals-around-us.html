Plants and Animals Around Us

Plants and animals are all around us in our homes, parks, gardens, and forests. They play important roles in keeping nature balanced. Plants provide food and oxygen, while animals help in pollination, spreading seeds, and keeping the environment healthy.

Understanding how plants and animals live and depend on each other helps us protect nature better.

Example: Bees collect nectar from flowers and help in pollination. Birds eat fruits and spread seeds to grow new plants.
Plants produce oxygen
Animals breathe oxygen
Animals help spread seeds
Seeds grow into new plants

Plants

  • Rose - A beautiful flowering plant
  • Mango Tree - Provides delicious fruit
  • Grass - Covers ground and provides food for animals

Animals

  • Butterflies - Help pollinate flowers
  • Birds - Spread seeds and build nests
  • Ants - Help aerate soil and recycle nutrients
