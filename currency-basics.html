Currency Basics

Currency is the money used in different countries to buy things. Each country has its own special currency with unique names and symbols.

Popular World Currencies

  • ₹ Indian Rupee (India)
  • $ US Dollar (United States)
  • € Euro (European Union)
  • £ British Pound Sterling (United Kingdom)
  • ¥ Japanese Yen (Japan)
  • ₩ South Korean Won (South Korea)
  • ₽ Russian Ruble (Russia)
  • C$ Canadian Dollar (Canada)
  • A$ Australian Dollar (Australia)
  • ₺ Turkish Lira (Turkey)

Coins and Notes

Currency comes in coins and notes (paper money). Coins are usually smaller amounts, and notes are bigger amounts.

People use coins and notes to buy food, toys, clothes, and many other things.

Learning about currency helps us understand money and how to use it wisely!
