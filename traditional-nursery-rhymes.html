Traditional Nursery Rhymes

Nursery rhymes are short, simple poems or songs for children. They have been told and sung for many years and teach us about language, rhythm, and fun stories.

These rhymes help children learn new words and enjoy music. They often tell stories about animals, nature, or daily life in a playful way.

Example:
“Twinkle, twinkle, little star,
How I wonder what you are!”
This rhyme teaches children about stars in the sky in a fun and musical way.
