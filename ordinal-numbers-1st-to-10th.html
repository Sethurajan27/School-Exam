Ordinal Numbers 1st to 10th

Ordinal numbers tell us the position or order of things, like first, second, third, and so on.

What are Ordinal Numbers?

They are used to show the place of something in a list or sequence.

Example:

In a race, the runner who finishes first gets the 1st place, the second gets the 2nd place, and the third gets the 3rd place.
