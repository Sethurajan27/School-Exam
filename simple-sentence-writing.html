Simple Sentence Writing

A sentence is a group of words that tells a complete thought. It usually has two important parts: a subject and a predicate.

Subject: tells who or what the sentence is about.
Predicate: tells what the subject does or is.

Example Sentences:
- The cat runs.
- She is happy.
- Birds fly in the sky.

Flowchart: How to Write a Simple Sentence

Think of Subject → Think of Action/Description → Put them together → Add a period → Sentence done!
