Money: Coins & Notes

Money is what we use to buy things like food, toys, and clothes. It helps us trade and get what we need every day.

Coins

Coins are small pieces of metal money. They come in different sizes and values.

  • Coins usually have numbers or pictures on them.
  • They are used for smaller amounts of money.
  • Examples include 1 rupee, 5 rupees, 10 cents, and 25 cents coins.

Notes (Paper Money)

Notes are pieces of paper money that have bigger values than coins.

  • Notes have pictures of important people, buildings, or symbols.
  • They come in different colors and sizes.
  • Examples include ₹10, ₹50, $1, $5, €10, and £20 notes.

Knowing about coins and notes helps us understand money better and use it wisely.
