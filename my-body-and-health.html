My Body and Health

Our body is made up of many parts like the head, arms, legs, and more. Keeping our body healthy helps us grow strong and happy.

Good health means eating nutritious food, exercising, sleeping well, and staying clean.

Example:
I drink water, eat fruits and vegetables, play outside, and wash my hands to stay healthy.

Flowchart: How to Stay Healthy

Eat Healthy Food → Exercise Daily → Get Enough Sleep → Keep Clean → Stay Healthy

Diagram: Parts of the Body

  • Head
  • Eyes, Ears, Nose, Mouth
  • Arms and Hands
  • Legs and Feet
  • Heart and Lungs (inside the body)

Health Tip Formula

Healthy Body = Good Food + Exercise + Sleep + Cleanliness
