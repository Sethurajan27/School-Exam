Food

Food is what we eat to stay healthy and strong. It gives us energy to play, learn, and grow.

Food comes in many types such as fruits, vegetables, grains, dairy, and proteins.

Example:
"Apples and bananas are fruits. Carrots and spinach are vegetables. Rice and bread are grains."

Diagram: Types of Food

  • Fruits (e.g., apple, banana)
  • Vegetables (e.g., carrot, spinach)
  • Grains (e.g., rice, bread)
  • Dairy (e.g., milk, cheese)
  • Proteins (e.g., eggs, meat)

Flowchart: How Food Helps Us

Eat Food → Gain Energy → Play and Learn → Grow Strong
