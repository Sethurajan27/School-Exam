House and Home

A house is a building where people live. It provides shelter and protection from weather, animals, and other dangers. A home is more than just a house; it is where you feel safe, loved, and comfortable.

People decorate their homes with furniture, pictures, and other things to make it cozy and personal.

Example: Your house may have many rooms like a bedroom, kitchen, and living room, but your home is where you share happy moments with your family.

Flowchart: From House to Home

House → Shelter → Safety → Comfort → Home

Diagram: Parts of a House

  • Roof - protects from rain and sun
  • Walls - provide structure and safety
  • Doors and windows - allow entry and light
  • Rooms - spaces for different activities
