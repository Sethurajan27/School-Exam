Letter Formation

Letter formation means writing letters in the correct way. Each letter has a special shape made by joining strokes.

To write letters correctly, follow the steps carefully.

Example:

Start at the top, draw a straight line down for the letter "l".
For the letter "o", start at the top and make a round circle.
For the letter "t", draw a straight line down and then a short line across at the top.

Flowchart: Steps to Form a Letter

Start → Hold Pencil Correctly → Follow Stroke Order → Draw Letter Shape → Check Letter Shape → Finish
