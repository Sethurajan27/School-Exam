Festivals and Traditions

Festivals and traditions are special celebrations that bring people together. They help us remember our culture, history, and important events.

Traditions are customs passed down from our family and community. Festivals often include music, dance, food, and fun activities.

Example:
During Diwali, people light lamps and share sweets to celebrate the victory of light over darkness.
