Food We Eat

Food is what we eat every day to stay healthy and strong. Different foods give us energy, help us grow, and keep our body working well.

Foods come from different sources like plants, animals, and even the earth. We eat fruits, vegetables, grains, meat, dairy, and many other types of food.

Example: An apple is a fruit that grows on a tree, while milk comes from cows. Both are food but come from different sources.
