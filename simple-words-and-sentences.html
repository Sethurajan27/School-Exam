Simple Words and Sentences

Words are small parts of language. When we put words together, they make sentences. Sentences tell us about people, things, and actions.

A simple sentence usually has a subject (who or what the sentence is about) and a verb (the action).

Example 1 (Word): Cat
Example 2 (Simple Sentence): The cat runs.
