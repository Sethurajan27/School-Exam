Time: Clock, Days & Months

Time helps us organize our day and understand when things happen. We use clocks, days, and months to keep track of time.

The Clock

The clock shows us the time in hours and minutes. It helps us know when to start or finish activities.

  • The short hand shows the hour.
  • The long hand shows the minutes.
  • Example: When the short hand is on 3 and the long hand is on 12, it is 3 o’clock.

Days

A day has 24 hours. We use days to count the time it takes for the earth to spin once.

  • There are 7 days in a week: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, and Sunday.
  • Each day is special and helps us plan activities.

Months

Months are groups of days. There are 12 months in a year.

  • Each month has about 30 or 31 days, except February which has 28 or 29 days.
  • Months help us know the time for seasons, holidays, and birthdays.

Understanding clocks, days, and months helps us manage our time well every day!
