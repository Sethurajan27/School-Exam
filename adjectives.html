Adjectives

An adjective is a word that describes a noun. It tells us more about a person, place, or thing.

Adjectives tell us about the size, color, shape, number, and feelings of a noun.

Example:
"The red apple is big. She has a happy smile."

Diagram: Types of Adjectives

  • Color: red, blue, green
  • Size: big, small, tall
  • Shape: round, square, long
  • Number: one, two, many
  • Feelings: happy, sad, angry

Flowchart: How to Use Adjectives

Noun → Add Adjective → Describe Noun
