Plants and Their Uses

Plants are very important to us because they provide many useful things. They give us food, medicine, clothes, and materials to build houses and make furniture.

Plants also help keep our environment clean by producing oxygen and providing homes for animals.

Example:
  • Fruits like apples and bananas are food plants.
  • Neem and tulsi plants are used in medicines.
  • Cotton plants give us cotton to make clothes.
  • Wood from trees like teak and pine is used to make furniture and houses.

We should take care of plants and plant more trees to keep our Earth healthy and green.
