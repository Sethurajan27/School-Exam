The Thirsty Crow

It was a hot summer day. A crow was very thirsty and flew in search of water. After a long time, he found a pot with very little water at the bottom.

The crow tried to drink the water but could not reach it. He looked around and found some small pebbles nearby. The clever crow started dropping the pebbles one by one into the pot. Slowly, the water level rose.

At last, the water came up enough for the crow to drink. He quenched his thirst and flew away happily.

Example:
Just like the crow didn’t give up and used his brain, we can solve problems with patience and smart thinking.

Flowchart: Story Steps

Thirsty Crow → Searching for Water → Finds Pot with Low Water → Drops Pebbles → Water Rises → Drinks Water → Happy Crow

Diagram: Scene Illustration

  • Crow: Smart and thirsty
  • Pot: Half-filled with water
  • Pebbles: Used to raise water level

Moral: Where there is a will, there is a way.
