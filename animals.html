Animals

Animals are living creatures that can move, eat, and breathe. They live in many places like forests, farms, and homes.

Animals can be big or small, wild or tame. They help us in many ways, such as giving us food, wool, and companionship.

Example:
"A cow gives us milk, and a dog is a friendly pet that plays with us."
