Measurement Basics

Measurement helps us understand how big, heavy, or full something is. We measure things to know their size, weight, or amount.

What Can We Measure?

We can measure many things like length (how long or tall something is), weight (how heavy it is), and volume (how much space it takes, especially liquids).

Units of Measurement

Length is measured in centimeters (cm), meters (m), or inches.

Weight is measured in grams (g) or kilograms (kg).

Volume is measured in liters (L) or milliliters (mL).

Why is Measurement Important?

Measurement helps us compare things. For example, to know which book is bigger, which fruit is heavier, or how much water to pour.

It helps us in daily life, school, cooking, and many other activities.
