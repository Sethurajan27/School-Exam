Nouns

A noun is the name of a person, place, animal, or thing. Nouns help us identify the world around us.

There are different kinds of nouns:

Types of Nouns

  • Common Nouns: Names of general things (e.g., boy, city, dog)
  • Proper Nouns: Names of specific people or places (e.g., John, London)
  • Animal Nouns: Names of animals (e.g., cat, lion)
  • Thing Nouns: Names of things (e.g., book, table)
Example:
"The dog plays in the park.
Sarah lives in London."
