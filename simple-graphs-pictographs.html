Simple Graphs & Pictographs

Graphs help us understand and show information in a simple way. A pictograph uses pictures or symbols to show data.

What is a Pictograph?

A pictograph uses pictures to represent numbers. Each picture stands for a certain number of things.

Example:

Look at this pictograph showing how many fruits children ate in a week.
