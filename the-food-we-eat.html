The Food We Eat

Food is what we eat to give our body energy and help it grow. Healthy food makes us strong and keeps us active.

Food comes from plants and animals. We eat fruits, vegetables, grains, milk, meat, and more.

Example:
Eating fruits like apples and bananas gives vitamins and energy to play and study.

Flowchart: How Food Helps Our Body

Food → Digestion → Nutrients Absorbed → Energy + Growth + Health

Nutrition Formula

Healthy Body = Balanced Food + Regular Exercise + Plenty of Water
