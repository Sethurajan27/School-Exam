The World of Animals

Animals are living creatures found everywhere around us — on land, in water, and even in the air. They come in many shapes, sizes, and types. Animals help keep nature balanced and provide us with many useful things.

Animals are classified into groups based on their features. For example, some animals have fur and feed their babies with milk, while others have scales and live in water.

Example: A dog is a mammal because it has fur and feeds its young with milk. A fish is different because it lives in water and has scales.
