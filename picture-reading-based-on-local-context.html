Picture Reading Based on Local Context

Picture reading helps us understand and describe what is happening around us in our local environment. We observe carefully and talk about the people, places, animals, and activities we see.

When we read a picture, we think about our daily life and what we know about our surroundings.

Example:
"In the picture, children are playing near a village well. There are trees around, and a cow is resting in the shade. The sun is shining brightly."

Flowchart: How to Read a Picture

Observe the Picture → Identify People and Animals → Notice the Place and Objects → Describe the Actions and Scene

Diagram: Elements to Include in Picture Reading

  • People and Their Activities
  • Animals Present
  • Places and Surroundings
  • Time of Day and Weather
