Understanding Positions: Top & Bottom

Positions tell us where something is placed compared to other things. Two common positions are top and bottom.

What Does "Top" Mean?

The top is the highest part or point of something.

What Does "Bottom" Mean?

The bottom is the lowest part or point of something.

Example:
Top
This box is at the top
Bottom
This box is at the bottom

In a stack of books, the book on the highest level is at the top, and the one on the lowest level is at the bottom.
