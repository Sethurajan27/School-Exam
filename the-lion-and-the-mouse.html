The Lion and The Mouse

Once upon a time, a lion was sleeping in the forest. A little mouse was playing nearby and accidentally ran over the lion’s nose. The lion woke up angrily and caught the mouse.

The mouse begged the lion to let him go and promised to help someday. The lion laughed but let him go. A few days later, the lion was caught in a hunter’s net. The little mouse came and gnawed the ropes with his sharp teeth, setting the lion free.

Example:
This story shows that even small creatures can be of help to big ones. We should be kind and helpful.

Flowchart: Story Flow

Lion sleeps → Mouse disturbs → Lion catches Mouse → Mouse begs → Lion frees Mouse → Lion trapped → Mouse helps → Lion free

Diagram: Characters and Action

  • Lion: Strong but kind
  • Mouse: Small but helpful
  • Hunter’s Net: Problem
  • Mouse's Teeth: Solution
Moral: No one is too small to help others. Always be kind and never underestimate anyone.
