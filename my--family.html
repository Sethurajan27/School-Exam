My Family

Family is a group of people who love and take care of each other. Our family can be small or big, and it includes parents, brothers, sisters, grandparents, and sometimes cousins and uncles.

Families help us learn good manners, share happy moments, and support us when we need help.

Example:
“My family has four members — my mother, my father, my younger sister, and me. We enjoy eating meals together and playing games on weekends.”
