Air Around Us

Air is all around us. It is invisible, but we can feel it when the wind blows. Air is made up of different gases that plants, animals, and people need to live.

Air helps us breathe and keeps the Earth warm. It also helps birds and airplanes to fly.

Example: When you blow up a balloon, you fill it with air. Without air, the balloon would stay flat.
