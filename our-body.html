Our Body

Our body is made up of many parts, each having a unique function. Together, they help us live, learn, play, and grow. It is important to understand the parts of our body to take good care of them.

Example: We see with our eyes, speak with our mouth, and walk using our legs. Every part is important.

Main Body Parts

  • Eyes - For seeing
  • Ears - For hearing
  • Hands - For holding and writing
  • Legs - For walking and running
  • Mouth - For speaking and eating

How to Take Care

  • Keep your body clean
  • Eat healthy food
  • Exercise daily
  • Sleep well
