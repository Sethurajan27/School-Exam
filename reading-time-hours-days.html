Reading Time (Hours & Days)

Time helps us know when things happen and how long they take. We measure time using hours and days.

What is an Hour?

An hour is a unit of time. There are 60 minutes in one hour.

  • Example: School starts at 9:00 AM and ends at 3:00 PM. That is 6 hours of school.
  • We use clocks to tell the hour and minute.

What is a Day?

A day has 24 hours. It starts at midnight and ends the next midnight.

  • Example: We sleep at night and wake up in the morning of a new day.
  • Days help us keep track of dates and events.

Learning to read the clock and understand hours and days helps us manage our daily activities better!
