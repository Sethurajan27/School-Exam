Transport and Communication

Transport helps us move people and goods from one place to another. Communication helps us share information with others near or far.

Different types of transport include walking, bicycles, cars, buses, trains, ships, and airplanes.

Communication can be done through letters, phones, internet, television, and radio.

Example: A postman delivers letters by transport, and we use phones to talk to our family far away.
