Our Body and Its Needs

Our body needs many things to grow, stay strong, and remain healthy. These needs help us do daily activities like studying, playing, and sleeping well. We should understand these needs and follow good habits every day.

Example: Just like a plant needs water, sunlight, and air to grow, our body also needs food, water, rest, and exercise to stay fit.

Food

  • Gives us energy
  • Helps us grow
  • Keeps us healthy

Water

  • Quenches thirst
  • Helps in digestion
  • Keeps our body cool

Exercise

  • Makes muscles strong
  • Keeps us active
  • Improves mood

Rest

  • Relaxes our body
  • Gives time to recover
  • Helps us grow
