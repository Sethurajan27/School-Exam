Animals Around Us

Animals live all around us—in our homes, gardens, farms, forests, and rivers. They are important because they help the environment stay healthy and provide us with many useful things.

We can find different types of animals based on where they live and how they behave. Some live on land, some in water, and others can fly in the sky.

Example: A dog is a land animal that lives with people, while a fish lives in water. Birds like sparrows fly in the air.
