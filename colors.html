Colors

Colors make the world beautiful. We see colors everywhere— in flowers, clothes, food, and nature. Each color has its own name and meaning.

There are many colors, but some common ones are red, blue, yellow, green, orange, purple, black, and white.

Example:
"The red rose looks bright and beautiful. The green leaves help the plant grow."
