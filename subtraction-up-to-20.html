Subtraction up to 20

In this topic, we learn how to subtract numbers up to 20.

Use your fingers, pictures, or objects to help you subtract. You can also count backward!

🍌 15 − 7 = 8

🎈 18 − 9 = 9

🍎 12 − 4 = 8

🧃 19 − 6 = 13

🎁 20 − 10 = 10

💡 Tip: Start with the bigger number and count back.
Example: 17 − 5 = ? Count: 16, 15, 14, 13, 12 → So, 17 − 5 = 12.

Practice with different number pairs daily to get strong in subtraction!

Subtraction tells us how many are left after taking some away!
