The Human Body

The human body is made up of many parts that work together to help us live, grow, and stay healthy. Each part of the body has a special job. Some help us move, some help us breathe, and others help us digest food or think clearly.

Example: We use our hands to write, pick up things, and eat. We use our legs to walk, run, and jump.

Sense Organs

  • Eyes - Help us see
  • Ears - Help us hear
  • Skin - Helps us feel
  • Tongue - Helps us taste
  • Nose - Helps us smell

Internal Organs

  • Heart - Pumps blood
  • Lungs - Help us breathe
  • Stomach - Helps digest food
  • Brain - Helps us think
