The World of Plants

Plants are living things that grow in soil, water, and air. They provide us with oxygen, food, and shelter. Plants have roots, stems, leaves, and flowers, each serving an important purpose.

Plants use sunlight to make their own food through a process called photosynthesis. This helps them grow and stay healthy.

Example: Trees like mango and banyan, flowers like rose and sunflower, and vegetables like spinach and carrot are all plants.

Plants are important to life on Earth because they help clean the air and provide food and homes for many animals.
