My Home

A home is a place where we live with our family. It gives us comfort, safety, and love.

Homes can be different types like apartments, houses, or cottages. Every home has rooms like a kitchen, bedroom, bathroom, and living room.

Example:
My home has a big kitchen where my mother cooks delicious meals, a cozy living room, and a garden outside.
