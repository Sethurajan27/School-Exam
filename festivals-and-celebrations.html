Festivals and Celebrations

Festivals and celebrations are joyful occasions where people come together to enjoy special moments. They help us express happiness, share culture, and strengthen bonds.

Celebrations can include music, dance, food, games, and colorful decorations.

Example:
On New Year’s Day, families celebrate with fireworks, parties, and wishes for a happy year ahead.
