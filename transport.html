Transport

Transport means moving people or goods from one place to another. It helps us travel and send things quickly and easily.

There are different types of transport:

  • Land Transport: Cars, buses, bicycles, and trains.
  • Water Transport: Boats and ships.
  • Air Transport: Airplanes and helicopters.
Example:
We use a bus to go to school, a ship to send goods across the sea, and an airplane to visit faraway places.
