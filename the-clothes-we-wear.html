The Clothes We Wear

Clothes are one of the basic needs of human beings. They protect us from weather conditions like heat, cold, and rain. Clothes also help us look neat and tidy.

We wear different clothes in different seasons:

  • Summer: Light cotton clothes to stay cool.
  • Winter: Woolen clothes to keep warm.
  • Rainy Season: Raincoats and waterproof boots to stay dry.

We also wear special clothes for school (uniforms), festivals, and special occasions.
