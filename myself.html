Myself

We are all special and unique individuals. "Myself" means who I am, what I like, and what makes me different from others. Learning about myself helps me understand my feelings, my family, my hobbies, and my dreams.

Knowing myself helps me make good choices and be kind to others.

Example:
I like playing football and drawing. My favorite color is blue. I have two brothers and one sister.
