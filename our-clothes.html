Our Clothes

Clothes are what we wear every day. They keep us comfortable, protect us from weather, and help us express our style. Different clothes are used for different occasions and seasons.

Clothes can be made from various materials like cotton, wool, silk, and synthetic fibers.

Example: In summer, we wear light cotton clothes to stay cool, and in winter, we wear woolen clothes to keep warm.
