Gender

Gender tells us whether a person or animal is male or female.

Types of Gender

  • Masculine: Male persons or animals (e.g., boy, father, rooster)
  • Feminine: Female persons or animals (e.g., girl, mother, hen)
  • Common: Used for both males and females (e.g., doctor, teacher)
  • Neuter: For things that are not male or female (e.g., table, book)
Examples:
  • Masculine: Man, King, Bull
  • Feminine: Woman, Queen, Cow
  • Common: Child, Teacher, Friend
  • Neuter: Chair, Car, Tree
