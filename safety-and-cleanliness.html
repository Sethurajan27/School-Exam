Safety and Cleanliness

Safety means protecting ourselves from danger, while cleanliness means keeping our surroundings free from dirt and germs. Both are important to stay healthy and happy.

We should always keep our home, school, and environment clean to avoid sickness and accidents.

Example: Washing hands before eating keeps germs away and prevents illness.
