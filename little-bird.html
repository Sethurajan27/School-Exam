Little Bird

A little bird is a small, beautiful creature with wings and feathers. Birds can fly in the sky and sing lovely songs. They live in nests made of twigs and leaves.

Birds eat seeds, insects, and fruits. Watching birds helps us learn about nature and makes us happy.

Example: The little sparrow chirped happily on the tree branch, welcoming the bright morning.
