Greetings

Greetings are words or actions we use to say hello or show respect when we meet someone.

Examples of Greetings:
- Hello!
- Good morning!
- How are you?
- Nice to meet you!
- Goodbye!

Flowchart: When to Use Greetings

Meet someone → Say Hello → Ask How Are You? → Have a nice talk → Say Goodbye

Common Greetings

  • Hello - General greeting
  • Good morning - Used in the morning
  • Good afternoon - Used in the afternoon
  • Good evening - Used in the evening
  • Goodbye - When leaving
