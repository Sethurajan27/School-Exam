The House We Live In

A house is a place where we live with our family. It gives us shelter, safety, and comfort. Houses can be made of different materials like bricks, wood, mud, or concrete.

Houses have different rooms such as bedrooms, kitchen, living room, and bathroom. Each room has a special use to make our life easy and comfortable.

Example: In a typical house, the kitchen is where food is prepared, and the bedroom is where we sleep and rest.
